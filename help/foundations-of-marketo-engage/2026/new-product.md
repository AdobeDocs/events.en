# Foundations of Marketo Engage: Announce a new product or feature

In this session, you’ll take what you’ve learned so far and apply it to a real use case: launching a product announcement. This builds on Core Concepts I by introducing more practical ways to use automation, targeting, and testing in your programs.

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3492620/?learn=on&enablevpops)

## Speakers

* Mel Lab-Kimball - Marketing Project Manager @ arrivia  
* Allison McIntyre - Digital Marketing Consultant

## Session Overview

Product announcements in Marketo
*   One-time sends vs. repeatable campaign setups

Campaign program setup
*   Channels, statuses, and keeping programs organized

Targeting with Smart Lists
*   Using filters vs. manual lists and avoiding over-targeting

Intro to A/B testing
*   What to test as a beginner (and what to skip for now)

Success metrics
*   Opens vs. clicks and how to think about performance

## Target Audience

New Adobe Marketo Engage users who are comfortable building a basic email and ready to start targeting, testing, and structuring more intentional campaigns.
