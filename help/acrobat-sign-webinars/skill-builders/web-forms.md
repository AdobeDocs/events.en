# Getting Started with Web Forms in Acrobat Sign

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3491645/?learn=on&enablevpops)

Still emailing forms back and forth or chasing down signatures one agreement at a time? Web Forms in Acrobat Sign let you publish a single signable form that anyone can access, fill, and sign — from your website, a shared link, or an embedded page — without you sending a thing. Watch this hands-on webinar where our experts cover:

* What are Web Forms and where do they shine – From HR teams publishing new-hire onboarding packets that hundreds of employees can complete, to Finance teams posting customer-facing application on a website to collect signatures at scale, to so much more!
* How to create and launch a Web Form – Watch our live walkthrough on how to create your first web form, including: uploading your document or template, placing form fields, configuring signer authentication, and publishing your form as a shareable URL or embeddable code.
* How to manage and track your Web Form results — Monitor submissions in real time, download form field data, edit or disable a live form, and keep your Web Forms organized as usage grows.

Whether you're collecting signatures from ten people or ten thousand, you'll leave this session ready to create, publish, and manage your first Web Form with confidence.
