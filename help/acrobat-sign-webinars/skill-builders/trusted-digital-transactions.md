# Trusted Digital Transactions with Adobe Acrobat Sign and OneID

As organizations across EMEA continue to digitize customer onboarding and agreement processes, one challenge remains: how do you ensure the person signing is really who they claim to be, without creating friction or delaying business? This challenge is particularly relevant for organizations operating in regulated industries or conducting transactions with customers, employees, or partners located throughout Europe, the Middle East, and Africa.

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3492866/?learn=on&enablevpops)

Join Adobe and OneID for this practical webinar to discover how identity verification and electronic signatures can work together to create trusted digital transactions that are faster, more secure, and easier for customers to complete. Our experts will cover:

* Why trusted digital transactions matter — Explore the growing challenges of identity fraud, customer onboarding, compliance, and remote engagement across EMEA, particularly in financial services, insurance, HR, and other regulated industries, and why identity verification is becoming a critical part of digital signing workflows.
* How Adobe Acrobat Sign and OneID work together — Learn how OneID identity verification integrates with Acrobat Sign through Adobe’s Digital Identity Gateway to authenticate signers as they electronically sign, creating higher-trust digital transactions with stronger identity assurance.
* Eliminating friction with verified data and forms — See how verified identity data can automatically populate form fields within Acrobat Sign agreements, reducing manual data entry, minimizing errors, improving completion rates, and delivering a better signer experience.
* A live demonstration and real-world use cases — Watch a complete workflow showing signer authentication with OneID, automatic form population using verified identity data, document signing, and the resulting audit trail containing identity verification evidence.

Whether you’re an Acrobat Sign administrator looking for stronger authentication options, a business leader responsible for customer onboarding, or a compliance professional seeking more trusted digital processes, you’ll leave with practical ideas for reducing fraud, accelerating onboarding, and improving customer experience. We’ll close with a live Q&A.
