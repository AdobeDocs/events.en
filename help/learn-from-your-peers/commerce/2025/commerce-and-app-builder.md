# Learn from your peers webinar: Adobe Commerce & App Builder Essentials - From Basics to Impact

Join Adobe Commerce Champion Alex Lyzun for an educational session exploring the future of Adobe Commerce and the essentials of extensibility with App Builder. In this session, Alex shares expert insights from his personal experience into how running cloud-native applications for Adobe Commerce can help lower cost of ownership & upgrades, integrate with all of your enterprise systems, and empower your business by offering flexibility, agility, and customization to meet your unique business needs.

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3476691/?learn=on&enablevpops)

## What you'll learn

* An overview of App Builder, migration with modules, and their value in driving greater speed, flexibility, and customization for your business
* How accelerators are best practice in flexible integration and faster time to market
* The role of scalable serverless actions in supporting growth and performance
* How event architecture like event subscription can enhance responsiveness and integration across your commerce ecosystem
* Key concepts behind setting up your environment

Whether you are just starting out with App Builder or are looking to understand the strategic value of extensibility, this webinar is designed to equip you with a clear, accessible foundation to help you unlock new possibilities with Adobe Commerce!
