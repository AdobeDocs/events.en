# Getting AI Right in AEM & EDS: Secure, Scalable Patterns for Developers and Admins

Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) Champion Krishna Kalyan Gorthi presents secure and scalable AI design patterns tailored for developers and admins. This session emphasizes responsible AI adoption within AEM environments, breaking down strategies into actionable takeaways. Key topics include:

* Differentiating AEM and EDS architecture for AI integration.
* Utilizing out-of-box AI features like smart tags and translations.
* Employing agents for automated tasks and governance.
* Creating custom AI integrations with enterprise-grade security.

The presentation outlines practical steps to implement AI responsibly, ensuring data security and compliance with enterprise standards. A decision matrix and readiness framework are provided to guide organizations in selecting the appropriate tools based on user roles and requirements.

Business outcomes include enhanced operational efficiency, secure AI adoption, and improved scalability across AEM and EDS platforms. An embedded video provides further insights from the session.
