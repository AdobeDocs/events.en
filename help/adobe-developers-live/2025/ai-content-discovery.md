# AI Driven Content Discovery with Channel Ready Renditions

Discover how AI transforms content discovery with Adobe Experience Manager. Learn to use Content Advisor for semantic search and AI-powered asset recommendations. This session covers campaign brief-based discovery and rapid access to Dynamic Media renditions, simplifying content reuse and delivery through an OpenAPI stack.

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3477673/?learn=on&enablevpops)

This session — AI Driven Content Discovery with Channel Ready Renditions — features Matt Briscoe, Deepa Gupta, and Mathias Siegel showing how developers can use Content Advisor to discover assets and content fragments with semantic search and AI-powered recommendations. Learn how campaign brief-based discovery and rapid access to Dynamic Media renditions across Scene7 and DM simplify content reuse and delivery through an OpenAPI stack. Recorded live from San Jose.
