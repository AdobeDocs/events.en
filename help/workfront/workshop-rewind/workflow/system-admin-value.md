# Own Your Impact as a Workfront System Admin

Join Nuria Munguia, Enterprise Workfront Platform Lead & Product Owner, from TD Bank as she shares how simple, but strategic Workfront configurations can create powerful visibility into team impact and business value. 

>[!VIDEO](https://video.tv.adobe.com/v/3492616/?learn=on&enablevpops)

In this session, Nuria will walk through real examples of how small, intentional configurations can unlock visibility into cost savings, efficiency gains, and strengthen the role of the Workfront System Admin. Whether you're new to Workfront or a seasoned admin, you'll leave with practical ideas to better advocate for your work, uncover opportunities for growth, and become a more strategic partner within your organization.
